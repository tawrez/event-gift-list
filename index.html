How it works

1

Create your list

Add your event details and list the gifts you’d actually love. Each gift is tracked so the same thing is never bought twice.

2

Share the QR code

We generate a unique link and QR code for your event. Print it on your card or share it on WhatsApp with your guests.

3

Guests pick and pay

Guests scan the QR, see your list, choose one gift and pay securely. Once a gift is taken, it instantly shows as unavailable.

4

Verify gifts at the event

Every paid gift gets a unique code. At the event, you match the code and hand over the right gift to the right person—fully tracked.
