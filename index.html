how giftloop works

From wishlist to shareable QR

Instead of guests guessing what to buy, GiftLoop gives them one elegant link with gifts you’ve pre‑selected. It works beautifully on phones, so even relatives in family groups can scan and choose in a few taps.

1. You create your list
Use the form above to share event details and build your wishlist. Add as many items as you like – you’re always in control of what appears.
2. We curate & price it
Our team reviews your wishlist, assigns clear prices, groups similar gifts and prepares a polished registry page with a gold‑accented design.
3. Guests pick & gift easily
You share the link or QR. Guests choose any available gift within their budget, and once a gift is taken, it’s hidden or marked so others don’t buy the same thing again.
