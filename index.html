how giftloop works

From wishlist to QR in three simple steps

Instead of guessing what to buy, your guests receive one clear, elegant link with all the gifts you’ve pre‑selected. It works beautifully for weddings, baby showers, birthdays and any celebration where many people want to gift you something special.

1. You create your list
Use the form above to share details about your event and build your wishlist. Add as many items as you like – you’re always in control of what appears on your registry.
2. We curate & price it
Our team reviews your wishlist, assigns clear prices, groups similar gifts and prepares a polished registry page along with a QR code tailored to your celebration.
3. Guests pick & gift easily
You share the link or QR with friends and family. They choose any available gift within their budget, and once it’s selected, it’s marked as taken so others don’t buy the same thing again.
why hosts love this

Fewer duplicate gifts, more thoughtful memories

Traditional gifting often leads to multiple mixers, repeated dinner sets and decor you don’t have space for. A gift registry solves this by giving guests a guided list of things you truly need, while still allowing them to choose freely.

Perfect for big guest lists
When you have many invitees, managing gifts manually becomes chaotic. GiftLoop keeps everything in one place so each guest sees a clear, updated picture of what’s still available.
Comfortable for every budget
Because each gift has a visible price, guests can quietly pick something within their comfort zone – whether it’s a small gesture or a larger group gift.
Made for Indian celebrations
From traditional weddings and griha pravesh to intimate baby showers and birthdays, the experience feels familiar and respectful while adding just the right amount of modern convenience.
