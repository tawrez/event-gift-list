how giftloop works

From wishlist to QR in three simple steps

Instead of everyone guessing what to buy, GiftLoop gives your guests a single, live list of gifts that you have already approved. It feels like a modern wedding or event registry, but tailored for Indian celebrations and everyday family functions. [web:10][web:7]

1. You tell us what you want
Add as many items as you like – from home appliances and personal care to experiences, vouchers or even contribution‑based gifts. You stay fully in control of what appears on your registry.
2. We price, polish and publish
Our team reviews your wishlist, assigns fair prices, groups similar items and makes the list easy to browse for guests of all ages. Then we send you a beautiful link and QR for your event.
3. Guests gift without confusion
Guests scan the QR, pick any free item within their budget and pay online. Once an item is taken, it disappears or shows as “already gifted”, which naturally avoids unnecessary repetitions and last‑minute returns. [web:9][web:12]
why hosts love this

Fewer duplicate gifts, more meaningful memories

Traditional gifting often leads to five mixers, three dinner sets and a cupboard full of things you never use. A personal gift registry fixes that by giving guests a clear list of what you actually need, and updating it automatically as gifts are claimed. [web:10][web:9]

Reduce waste & repetition
Because your registry updates in real‑time, guests naturally spread out across the list. You receive a variety of useful gifts instead of piles of the same thing. [web:9][web:12]
Easier for your guests
Guests no longer have to call your family to ask “what should we bring?”. They can simply scan, choose a price point that feels comfortable and check out in a few taps. [web:9]
Perfect for Indian events
