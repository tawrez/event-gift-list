HOW GIFTLOOP WORKS

From wishlist to shareable QR code.

Instead of everyone guessing what to buy, your guests see a single curated list of gifts you’ve already approved — in a simple gold and cream interface that fits perfectly with wedding or baby‑shower invites.

1. You create your list
Tell us about your event and add the gifts you’d genuinely like to receive. You’re always in control of what appears on your registry.
2. We price & polish
Our team reviews your wishlist, sets clear prices, and prepares a neat registry page with a QR code and short link you can share.
3. Guests gift with ease
Guests scan the QR, choose any free gift within their budget, pay online, and we mark that item as taken to avoid unwanted duplicates.
WHY HOSTS LOVE IT

Fewer repeated gifts, more thoughtful memories.

Big Indian celebrations can easily lead to three dinner sets, four mixers and random decor that never leaves the box. GiftLoop keeps the emotional part of gifting, but quietly removes the waste and guesswork.

Perfect for large guest lists
One shared list keeps everyone aligned. Guests don’t have to call your relatives for suggestions or worry if someone already bought the same thing.
Comfortable for every budget
With clear pricing on each gift, friends and family can choose a contribution that matches what they’re comfortable spending.
Made for weddings & more
Works equally well for weddings, engagements, baby showers, birthdays, anniversaries and house‑warmings.
