How it works

1

Create your list

Enter your event name and add the gifts you’d love to receive. Each gift shows as available or taken so nothing gets repeated.

2

Share the QR code

We generate a unique link and QR code. Download it and print it on your invitation card or share it on WhatsApp.

3

Guests pick and pay

Guests scan the QR, see your list, choose one gift and pay securely. As soon as a gift is taken, it’s marked unavailable.

4

Gift verification at event

Every paid gift gets a unique code. At the event, you match the code and hand over the gift to the right guest.

Why we built this

Traditional gifting creates awkward moments—three people bring the same thing, someone spends a lot on something you’ll never use, and guests are always guessing what to buy.

This simple tool fixes that. You stay in control of your wishlist, guests feel confident about what they’re gifting, and the entire process is smooth, transparent and organised.
